Dementia Care Website

Dementia Care is a simulation website created for use by dementia patients and their families. It was an individual project for a Texts and Cultures of Science course taken at Temple University. The intention was to extract information from sites used by medical professionals and translate it into an empathetic language intended for a broader audience (in particular the patients and family of the patients) without compromising the effectiveness and accuracy of the content.
